The Safe Autonomous Organization Slider

How much do you trust your AI agent? The answer isn't yes or no — it's a stage. Click through the five-stage framework from Andon Labs and find where you actually are.

Click any stage to explore it

Where are you on this slider?

Most people are at different stages for different tasks. Locate yourself honestly.

Stage 1
"I review every ChatGPT response before sharing it"
Stage 2
"I let Claude write a whole essay then I edit it"
Stage 3
"I have it auto-respond to emails and check the inbox once a day"
Stage 4
"It manages my calendar and only alerts me on conflicts"

You might be at stage 3 for writing and stage 1 for anything touching money. That's healthy — it means you're thinking about trust per decision class, not AI use in general.
